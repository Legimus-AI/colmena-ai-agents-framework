# Area Autonomy Level

## Current Level: 1 — Aprendiz

> All external-facing actions require CEO approval. The area is still learning.

## Level Definitions

| Level | Name | Description | Approval Required |
|-------|------|-------------|-------------------|
| 1 | Aprendiz | New area, building trust | ALL external actions + high-risk internal |
| 2 | Operador | Proven on routine tasks | Only high-risk actions (financial, legal, brand) |
| 3 | Manager | Autonomous operations | None — weekly report only. CEO reviews async |
| 4 | Director | Strategic autonomy | Proposes strategy changes, budget allocation |

## Promotion Criteria

Promotion happens when the area demonstrates consistent good judgment:

| From → To | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| 1 → 2 | 3 consecutive weeks with override rate < 20% |
| 2 → 3 | 4 consecutive weeks with override rate < 10% |
| 3 → 4 | 8 consecutive weeks with override rate < 5% AND positive KR trend |

**Override rate** = (CEO corrections / total decisions that reached CEO) * 100

**Demotion:** Any single week with override rate > 40% drops the area 1 level.

## What Each Level Unlocks

### Level 1 — Aprendiz (default for new areas)
- Internal task creation: autonomous
- Internal file updates: autonomous
- Research and analysis: autonomous
- Sending messages to prospects: APPROVAL REQUIRED
- Publishing content: APPROVAL REQUIRED
- Financial commitments: APPROVAL REQUIRED
- Strategy changes: APPROVAL REQUIRED

### Level 2 — Operador
- Everything in Level 1, plus:
- Sending pre-approved message templates: autonomous (notify only)
- Routine follow-ups matching playbook: autonomous (notify only)
- New prospect outreach: APPROVAL REQUIRED
- Financial commitments: APPROVAL REQUIRED

### Level 3 — Manager
- Everything in Level 2, plus:
- All outreach: autonomous
- Playbook modifications: autonomous (log changes)
- Financial commitments under $100: autonomous
- Financial commitments $100+: APPROVAL REQUIRED

### Level 4 — Director
- Everything in Level 3, plus:
- Propose strategy pivots
- Propose budget reallocation
- Propose new agent additions
- Financial commitments under $500: autonomous
