## Company Summary

Bassika is a Peruvian women's casual fashion brand operating on Shopify. Legal name: **BSK RETAIL S.A.C.** (RUC: 20610199489), incorporated November 2022. The brand is owned/managed through **Bulos Retail**, which also manages the Bultaco brand (men's line). The Shopify internal handle is `maria-goes-online.myshopify.com`, using the "Dawn Bassika era by SPOT" theme.

Bassika positions itself as a brand that "inspires and empowers women through high-quality garments and timeless design." They sell Made-in-Peru casual fashion basics — coats, blazers, blouses, jeans, pants, dresses, footwear, and accessories.

They have **3 physical stores** in Lima plus a fully operational e-commerce site:
1. Av. Emilio Cavenecia 157, Miraflores (flagship)
2. C.C. Jockey Plaza - Nuevo Hall
3. C.C. La Rambla - San Borja
