## Company Summary

Jugueteria Mundo Magico de Regalos S.A.C. (trade name: "Mundo Magico de Regalos") is a wholesale and retail toy and gift distributor based in Chiclayo, Lambayeque, Peru. The SAC entity was registered with SUNAT on May 1, 2024 (RUC: 20612468461), though the business has operated since approximately 2015.
