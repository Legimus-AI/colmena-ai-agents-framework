## Research
- Hunter.io > Prospeo for .pe domains (4/5 success vs 1/5)
- Shopify storefront /products.json is PUBLIC — use for product count verification
- SUNAT Consulta RUC resolves E.I.R.L. titulares (search by razon social, not brand)
- Chrome DevTools MCP > WebFetch for site verification (WebFetch only reads 1 page)
- **BuiltWith Shopify list contains stale data:** 2/3 prospects from the Peru Shopify batch (Mundo Magico, Dulce Aroma) were NOT on Shopify. Recommend verifying platform BEFORE investing deep research time. Quick check: visit site + inspect for Shopify markers (myshopify.com handle, /products.json)
