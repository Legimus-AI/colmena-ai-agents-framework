# Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) — Legimus AI

## Primary Persona: "Mariana"

- **Role:** Female e-commerce owner/founder, 28-45 years old
- **Location:** Peru (primary), Chile (secondary)
- **Platform:** Shopify or WooCommerce
- **Products:** 20-200 SKUs, physical products (fashion, beauty, food, supplements)
- **Pain:** Receives 50-100+ daily WhatsApp inquiries, can't respond fast enough, loses sales
- **Current solution:** Manual WhatsApp replies, maybe WhatsApp Business basic
- **Budget:** $50-$200/month for tools
- **Trigger event:** Holiday season, viral post, new product launch → inquiry spike

Peru doesn't pay for "automation." They pay for "selling more."
Frame everything as revenue impact, not efficiency gains.
