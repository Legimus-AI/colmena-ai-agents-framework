# Sales Playbooks — Ventas

## Playbook 1: Outbound E-commerce (Cold → Warm)

### Sequence (3 messages, 7 days)

**Message 1 — Day 0 (Cold intro)**
- Subject: specific reference to their store/product
- Body: 1 pain point + 1 value prop + soft CTA
- Max 150 words
- Tone: casual, helpful, no corporate speak

**Message 2 — Day 3 (Value add)**
- Subject: Re: original
- Body: social proof or insight about their specific niche
- Include: "Vi que tu tienda tiene [X productos]... Nuestros clientes con catalogos similares [resultado]"
- Soft CTA: "Te cuento como funciona en 15 min?"

**Message 3 — Day 7 (Urgency)**
- Subject: new, with urgency angle
- Body: founding partner pricing (40% OFF), limited spots
- Hard CTA: calendar link or WhatsApp direct

### Rules
- ALWAYS personalize. Generic = delete.
- Lead with THEIR problem, not our product.
- Never mention competitors by name.
- Include specific numbers from their store (products, followers, etc.)

## Playbook 2: Demo Execution

### Pre-Demo (closer prepares)
1. Review account folder (overview + activity + artifacts)
2. Prepare: 3 pain points to validate, 2 features to demo, 1 closing question
3. Write demo brief in artifacts/demo-brief.md

### During Demo (Victor executes)
1. Discovery (5 min): validate pains, ask about current solution
2. Demo (10 min): show magic onboarding → live bot → WhatsApp conversation
3. Close (5 min): founding partner offer, next steps

### Post-Demo (closer documents)
1. Update accounts/<slug>/activity.md with demo notes
2. Create follow-up task for writer if proposal needed
3. Update pipeline stage

## Playbook 3: Objection Handling

See shared/OBJECTIONS.md for the full database.

Common objections:
- "Es muy caro" → Reframe as ROI: "Cuantas ventas pierdes al dia por no responder a tiempo?"
- "Ya tengo un chatbot" → "Legimus no es un chatbot. Es una empleada digital que consulta tu stock en tiempo real."
- "No tengo tiempo para configurar" → "Son 5 minutos. Te lo montamos en la demo."
